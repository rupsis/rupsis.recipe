I've tried a few different recipies for pickled red onions, and they have always come out too sweet. I think I've finally gotten the balance right. 


```
1 pound red onions, julienned
1 cups white wine vinegar
1 cup water
1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
3/4 table spoon kosher salt
```


Bring the vinegar, water, sugar, and salt to a simmer. Once everything has disollved, pour over the cut onions in a jar. Let cool down to room temperature, and store in the fridge for up to 3 months. 

Bonus: Add in some sliced jalapenios for a little kick.
