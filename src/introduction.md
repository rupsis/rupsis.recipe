# Introduction

I don't expect anyone to read this, but if you do, thanks. I hope I've had the pleasure of cooking for you. To me, cooking is one of the most rewarding activities. It's the perfect blend of craft, experimentation, science, and art. Being able to create something and share it with the people I love, and watch them enjoy it makes me happy. 

This book is a collection of things I enjoy, and recipies that I think are worth sharing. Some of them I've created myself, some of them are personal modifications to other recipeies, and some I've just straight of stole (Note: If this were to ever get published, this parentetical is a TODO for the editor to delete this admission of guilt).
